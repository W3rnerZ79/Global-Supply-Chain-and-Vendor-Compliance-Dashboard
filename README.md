# 📊 Supply Chain & Supplier Quality Dashboard

This interactive Power BI dashboard analyzes logistics efficiency and vendor compliance for a consumer goods supply chain across five major fulfillment hubs (**Bangalore, Chennai, Delhi, Kolkata, and Mumbai**) tracking **Cosmetics, Haircare, and Skincare** lines.

## 🔑 Key Features & Analytics

* **Executive Performance Tracking:** Consolidates high-level logistics metrics including **577.60K in Revenue Generated**, **243.86K in Inventory Value** (working capital), and a baseline **Average Lead Time of 17.08 days**.
* **Stockout Risk Analysis:** Features a custom 4-quadrant matrix mapping **Stock Levels vs. Lead Time** to instantly isolate high-risk items with low safety stock and prolonged vendor delays (over 15 days).
* **Supplier Quality Auditing:** Compares vendor throughput against quality risk, highlighting **Supplier 1** as a top performer (high volume with a low **1.80% defect rate**) and exposing **Supplier 4** as a critical operational risk due to a **66.67% inspection failure rate**.

## 🛠️ Technical Implementation

* **Risk Modeling:** Built a dynamic four-quadrant scatter plot using customized constant lines to bucket inventory safety thresholds.
* **Granular Cross-Filtering:** Integrated interactive slicers for *Product Type* and *Location* to enable regional managers to run targeted performance audits by specific city hubs.
* **Data Engineering:** Developed robust DAX measures to calculate transactional averages, compliance distributions, and product-specific defect frequencies.
